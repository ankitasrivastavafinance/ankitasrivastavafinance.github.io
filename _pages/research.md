## **Research Interests**
My research focus lies at the intersection of **Empirical Asset Pricing**, **Financial Risk Management**, and **Risk Premia**. I am particularly interested in using advanced econometric modeling and large-scale longitudinal datasets to understand market microstructure and risk-return dynamics.
